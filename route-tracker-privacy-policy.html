Privacy Policy

Route Travel Tracker · Last updated: June 2026

Data Storage

Route Travel Tracker stores data exclusively in your local browser storage (chrome.storage.local):

  • Your configured routes (origin and destination addresses)
  • Measured travel times with timestamps
  • Alert history

No data is ever transmitted to any external server. There are no user accounts, no tracking, and no analytics of any kind.

Google Maps

The extension accesses google.com/maps in short-lived background tabs to read current travel times. Google's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy apply to those requests. The extension does not store any Google account data.

Permissions

  • tabs / scripting: To open temporary Google Maps background tabs and read travel times from the page.
  • storage: To save measurements locally in your browser.
  • alarms: To trigger a measurement every 15 minutes while tracking is active.
  • notifications: To alert you when travel time is significantly above average.

Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.
