Privacy Policy

All extensions by kopitiful · Last updated: June 2026

Data Storage

Our extensions store data exclusively in your local browser storage (chrome.storage.local). This may include settings, preferences, and any data the extension needs to function.

No data is ever transmitted to any external server. There are no user accounts, no tracking, and no analytics of any kind.

Permissions

Each extension requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality. Permissions may include:

  • storage: To save settings and data locally in your browser.
  • tabs: To interact with or open browser tabs as required by the extension's purpose.
  • scripting: To inject scripts into pages the extension needs to read or modify.
  • alarms: To trigger scheduled background tasks.
  • notifications: To display browser notifications when relevant events occur.

Permissions are never used beyond the stated purpose of each extension.

Third Parties

Our extensions do not share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

External Websites

Some extensions may interact with external websites (e.g. to read publicly visible content). The privacy policies of those websites apply to those interactions. We do not store or transmit any data obtained from external websites to our own servers.
