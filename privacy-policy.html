Privacy Policy – Auto Load More

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Data Collection

Auto Load More does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, browsing history, or page content. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab / scripting – required to inject the click script into the currently active tab when you press "Start". No other tabs are ever accessed.

Third Parties

No data is shared with any third party. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue in the extension's repository or contact the developer directly.
